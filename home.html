Who We Are

Affordable Tree Trimming LLC is a fully licensed and insured arbor company based out of Oviedo, FL and servicing all of Central Florida.

Our Goal

Damage prevention is the centerpiece of our work. Removal of hazardous trees and limbs before the next big storm can save your property from costly damage. We offer this at an affordable price, with 100% satisfaction in our workmanship, customer service, and end product. Although a small company, we are able to handle any job from small regular trimming to removal of any hazardous, large trees and limbs. Call to schedule an estimate and we will take care of everything.
